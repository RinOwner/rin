Our Story

Why Solar Care Group exists

Solar and industrial sites depend on work that's easy to overlook until it's neglected — clean panels, controlled vegetation, maintained access roads, and a crew that shows up. Solar Care Group was built to own that work fully, so site teams can focus on operations rather than coordinating multiple contractors.

We operate as an Uzbekistan-based service company, structured to work directly alongside site management, under their safety rules and reporting lines.
