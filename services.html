01

Solar Panel Cleaning

Dust, sand and debris cut generation output over time. We run scheduled and on-demand cleaning cycles sized to your site's soiling rate and access constraints, using methods that protect panel coatings and seals.

  • — Scheduled cleaning cycles
  • — On-demand call-outs
  • — Water-conscious methods
  • — Post-clean condition checks
02

Vegetation & Grass Control

Uncontrolled growth blocks sun exposure, obstructs access roads, and creates fire risk. We manage ground cover across the array footprint and surrounding site on a set cycle.

  • — Routine mowing & clearing
  • — Herbicide programs where approved
  • — Access road & perimeter clearing
  • — Fire-risk vegetation management
03

Site Maintenance

General upkeep of fencing, drainage, signage, roads and site condition — the ongoing work that keeps a site audit-ready and safe to move around.

  • — Fencing & signage upkeep
  • — Drainage & erosion checks
  • — Access road condition
  • — General site housekeeping
04

Technical Support

Field-level support for routine inspections, minor repairs and equipment checks between full technical service visits.

  • — Visual inspections
  • — Minor repair support
  • — Equipment condition checks
  • — Issue reporting & escalation
05

Manpower Supply

Trained crews supplied and managed for short-term surges or long-term site presence, scaled to the work plan.

  • — Trained, briefed crews
  • — Short-term & long-term supply
  • — Site-specific onboarding
  • — Supervised on-site management
06

Transport & Logistics Support

Vehicles, equipment transport and logistics coordination so crews, materials and tools reach site on schedule.

  • — Crew & equipment transport
  • — Materials logistics
  • — Site-to-site coordination
  • — Scheduling around site access windows
